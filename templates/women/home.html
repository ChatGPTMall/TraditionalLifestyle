Elevating Luxury & Confidence

Vintage – The Premium Salon for Ladies was established in 2024 in Zirakpur with a mission to elevate luxury beauty services for women. What began as a vision quickly became a trusted brand for flawless, natural hair transformations and premium salon experiences.

We empower women through expert hair solutions, specializing in hair extensions, hair patches, and advanced chemical treatments using innovative techniques tailored to every need.
