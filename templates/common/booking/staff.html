Master Barber & Educator

Owner, Brooklyn Luxury Barbershop

Wajid Salmani is a UK GBBA (Great British Barbering Academy) certified barber with over 10 years of professional experience. He currently serves as an educator at Kingsman Barbershop and is recognized as a celebrity barber.

His training and mentorship experience spans across prestigious establishments including Goa Chop Shop Barber & Brand, New Tech Grooming Studio, Kings Men Barbershop Hyderabad, and Kings Men Barbershop Bangalore.

Wajid also delivers professional theory classes at CreativeZone International Salon and Academy, passing on his expertise to the next generation of barbers.

UK GBBA Certified Celebrity Barber Master Educator
{% else %}

CreativeZone International

Salon and Academy, Zirakpur

CreativeZone International Salon and Academy is our dedicated training facility where aspiring stylists learn the art of beauty. Located in Zirakpur, this academy is the foundation of our commitment to excellence.

Our academy offers comprehensive training programs in hair styling, extensions, coloring techniques, and chemical treatments. Students learn from industry experts and gain hands-on experience with premium products.

We believe in nurturing talent and creating professionals who can deliver the same level of excellence that defines the Vintage Salon experience.
